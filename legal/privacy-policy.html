Privacy Policy

Effective date: March 20, 2026

Audiorr ("the App") is a music player client that connects to your personal Navidrome server. Your privacy is important to us. This policy explains how the App handles your information.

1. Information We Collect

None. Audiorr does not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal data. The App does not use analytics, tracking, advertising, or telemetry of any kind.

2. How the App Works

Audiorr connects directly to a Navidrome-compatible server that you own and operate. All communication occurs exclusively between your device and your server over your local or private network. No data passes through our servers — we do not operate any servers.

3. Data Stored on Your Device

The App may store the following locally on your device:

  • Your server URL and authentication credentials (stored securely in device storage)
  • Cached album artwork and metadata for offline performance
  • Your playback preferences and settings

This data never leaves your device and is removed when you uninstall the App.

4. Third-Party Services

Audiorr does not integrate with any third-party analytics, advertising, or data-processing services.

5. Children's Privacy

The App does not collect data from anyone, including children under 13.

6. Changes to This Policy

If we update this policy, the revised version will be posted on this page with an updated effective date.
